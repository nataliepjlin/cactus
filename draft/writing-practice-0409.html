Question

Holidays honor people or events. If you could create a new holiday, what person or event would it honor and how would you want people to celebrate it? Use specific reasons and details to support your answer.

Answer

Aside from enabling people to have some days off, holidays are set to raise the attention of some special events or people. If I could create a new holiday, I would like to have a special holiday called “eco-friendly day”. The date would be the same as Earth Day, which falls on April 22th, and the holiday would be celebrated in the following two ways.

To start with, no plastic wastes such as bags or disposable utensils should be produced on that day. People need to bring their own containers or tablewares to purchase items, buy take outs, and so on. For the shopkeepers, they are also required not to provide any plastic bags, disposable spoons, or chopsticks. Instead, they have to offer customers reusable tablewares to ensure not producing wastes. The reason why this action is necessary is that people in Taiwan eat out very often, and for convenience, we usually use 2 to 3 containers, plastic chopsticks while ordering food deliveries, eating at food vendors, night markets et cetera. Day by day, tons of indecomposable trash are produced. It’s high time that we started cutting down the usage of plastic products and getting used to the inconvenience.

The second thing to do on this holiday is not to drive or ride motorcycles. During the shut down last summer, it is reported that air pollution had significantly improved. This shows that reducing the traffic amount is an effective way to alleviate the issue. In addition, forcing people to travel on foot or by bike can slow down the pace, so we can notice how crucial it is to lessen the harm to the environment. On top of that, we can also spend more time focusing on interacting with people around us, too.

To save our planet, we must take action. By creating a special holiday, we can cut down the usage of plastic products and reduce the emission of air pollution. Ultimately, we can raise the awareness of protecting our environment and make our world a better place.
